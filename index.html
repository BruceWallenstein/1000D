About 1000D

1000D is a webcomic written, drawn, and colorized by Bruce Wallenstein and Grant Rajesh. It began on June 3, 2022.
As a younger webcomic, we don't have a set posting plan yet, but we expect to post around Friday of every week. We may start posting bi-or-tri-weekly later on.
